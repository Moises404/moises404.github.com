Rembrandt is a MacBook Pro 
programmed with MaxMSP, Growler
and  Hardware Growler to 
personify a laptop. 

Using MaxMSP to access the 
camera of the computer, the
laptop reacts to movement
with soundbites from the Gun
Turret from Portal II.

Rembrandt also reacts when
someone plugs and unplugs the
charger, signaling Friend! 
and Why...
