ART404.COM 
Art404 is a new-media collective
comprised of Myself & Manuel Palou.
We've launched an online gallery 
called Low Budget Gallery.  

We showcase work in an online gallery
called Low Budget Gallery, and combine 
this method with photo manipulation, 
so sometimes we will place semi real
work in a semi real space and then make
it real via Photoshop. Due to the nature
of our art making, our work often deals
with legitimacy, value, perception, 
& deception.

Recently, we've shown at transmediale
2k+12in Berlin, Ars Electronica Festival
in Austria & Conflux Festival in NYC.
